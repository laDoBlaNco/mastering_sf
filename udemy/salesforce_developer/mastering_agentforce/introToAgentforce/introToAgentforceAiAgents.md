# MASTERING AGENTFORCE | 1: INTRODUCTION TO AGENTFORCE AND AI AGENTS | iNTRO TO AGENTFORCE

---

- **What is Agentforce**: Agentforce is a new platform developed by SF that allows businesses to create and deploy intelligent and autonomous AI Agents. 
        These agents work alongside human employees to enhance customer experiences and streamline business operations. SF provides out-of-the-box Agents for Sales, Service and Marketing, which we can just turn on, but customers can also extend these agents or build new agents as per the business needs. So the  **Agentforce Platform** provides the ability to Partners to build and Deploy agents and agetn actions through AppExchange which customers can purchase and add to their orgs as well. 
<br>

- **Agent**: Agents are AI assistants who can perform tasks, make decisions, and interact with customers or internal systems autonomously. These AI Agents understand and process information, communicate natrually with users, and take actions to perform specific tasks. It uses ***SF Atlas Reasoning Engine*** to understand the intent of the Questions and follow all the instructions defined for the particular task, find the appropriate response and action and then perform the task as requested.
    - Agentforce is a form of Agentic AI which is the 3rd phase of the AI movement:
        - Predictive AI
        - Generative AI
        - *Agentic AI*
    - **Example**:
        - **Customer Service Agents**:
        - **Scenario**: A customer wants to return a pair of shoes purchased online.
        - **AI Agent's Role**: The AI Agent assists the customer by accessing their purchase history, initiating the return process, and providing instructions for shipping, all without needing a human representative.
        - What It Does:
            - Answers questions, takes actions, follows instructions in everyday language
            - Works on multiple channels in SF. Agents for internal employees can help users in the daily tasks while an agent can be for customer services deployed on Community site, for a WhatsApp chat, for attending calls, etc. 
    - How it's different: It's not just a regular chatbot. While bots follow strict scripts, Copilot agents are more flexible and can handle a wide range of requests. It's built to understand complex instructions and carry out multiple tasks by itself.
<br>

- **Actions**: 
    - Comes with many built-in tasks it can do right away which are called *Standard Actions* (like drafting emails or finding customer records).
    - I can also customize it to do special tasks specific to my business needs (custom actions through Apex, flow and Prompt template, External APIs)
<br>

- **Trust and Security**:
    - Safety first: Agent makes sure only authorized users can see sensitive data (Respect user permissions)
    - It uses special architecture defined to keep data secure and private, following SF's high standards for trust and safety (**Einstein Trust Layer**)
<br>

- **Key Features**:
    - AI agents handle routine or repetitive tasks, allowing employees to focus on more strategic and creative work.
    - AI agents use data to provide customized experiences for each customer
    - AI agents can autonomously perform actions like answering customer inquiries, processing orders, or scheduling appointments
    - Businesses can easily build and deploy AI agents using tools like **Agent Builder** without needing advanced coding skills.
    - AgentForce includes safeguards to ensure AI agents operate responsibly and protect customer data.
